Experiment 1 - Hello Glitch/Generator

Male OC Creator!

Description

This generator helps pick traits and features for artists in order to create a male original character.

Technical

The generator uses lists of filler words pertaining to facial features, hairstyles, and personalities. The function replacer() is used by generate() to randomly select a word from the many lists of filler words. A template is formed containing slotPatterns which is then used by generate() in order to replace the slotPatterns with a word via replacer().

Reflection

This experiment was a success at generating unique male character designs. The process was not too difficult or too easy with only a few issues such as editing the look of the generator inside index.html or understanding how to integrate the generator into my portfolio.
