Experiment 2 - Living Impressions

Description

This project offers a generative, living impression of this inspiring image:

Technical

Relation to inspiring image:

Color - colors were taken from inspiring image using an [eyedropper tool](https://imagecolorpicker.com/en).

Primitives - rectangles were used for background, triangles for pine trees, and lines to simulate the Northern Lights.

Polygons - mountain was created using custom-shaped polygon involving noise() and random() in order to create more texture.

Generative - positioning of trees and mountains change as seed grows, as well as slight changes in Northern Lights positioning.

Life - Northern Lights moves across canvas in wavy pattern to simulate real world movement.

Reflection

This experiment was largely a success but also had room for improvement. Although I am proud that I was able to replicate the Alaskan Northern Lights as best with my current abilities and knowledge, I felt like with more time and research I could've added more details to my design. However, I am very happy with how my design turned out especially with my Northern Lights and if I had more time I would've done more with my mountain such as adding a snow layer on top and maybe subtle clouds in the background.
